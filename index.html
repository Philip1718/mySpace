Welcome to the Space Program

We’re thrilled to hear that you’re interested in becoming an astronaut! Space exploration is one of the most exciting and challenging adventures you can undertake. But before we start our journey, there are a few criteria you need to meet to qualify for this amazing opportunity. Becoming an astronaut isn’t just about dreaming big; it’s about meeting certain requirements and being ready for the challenges of space travel. Are you up for the adventure? Let’s explore what it takes to join our elite space program. Read on to find out if you have what it takes to reach for the stars!

The Criteria to Become an Astronaut

  • Enthusiasm for Learning New Skills: As an aspiring astronaut, you’ll need a genuine passion for acquiring new knowledge and skills. This includes understanding complex scientific concepts, mastering new technologies, and adapting to challenging environments.
  • Mastery of the Moon Walk: One of the exciting activities you'll learn is the moon walk. This iconic maneuver is essential for astronauts working on the lunar surface, and you'll need to perfect it to navigate effectively in reduced gravity.
  • Space Cooking Expertise: Cooking in space is different from cooking on Earth. You'll learn how to prepare unique space cuisine, including baking space worms—a fun and essential skill for long-duration missions where food preparation is key.
  • Spaceship Navigation Skills: Operating a spaceship requires a deep understanding of spacecraft systems and navigation techniques. You’ll train to handle the controls, navigate through space, and manage various onboard systems to ensure a safe journey.
